About me!

Web development

One of the passions I've recently discovered is web development and web design. In the past, I had never really considered these topics, but I started becoming passionate about them when I saw my boyfriend dedicating himself to web development. Today, I'm excited by the idea of creating something new, designing, and building from scratch. When I write code, time flies by, and I never want to stop. This passion has grown so much that I would love to turn it into a career.

Animals

I've always been an animal lover, a love that has grown over the years. Since I was a child, I’ve been surrounded by dogs, cats, and other animals, and I still share my life with them today. Every time I see an animal in distress, I feel sad and always try to help, whether by providing food or finding a shelter. Pastry making

My interest for pastry started a few years ago when, for birthdays and my sweet tooth, I began making cakes. Since then, I’ve never stopped baking, and today I’m trying to improve my technique, learning on my own to make more complex cakes. Pastry fascinates me not only for the creativity it allows but also for the precision needed to achieve a good result. Every dessert is a challenge, but the satisfaction of seeing it completed is priceless. In the future, I would love to take some courses to refine my skills and learn the secrets of professionals.
